## Background

- Adopted at birth, which instilled a sense of being "abandoned" and "chosen/special." Dropped out of Reed College but audited classes (calligraphy) that influenced his aesthetic. Traveled to India seeking enlightenment. Co-founded Apple in a garage. Ousted in 1985, founded NeXT and Pixar, returned to save Apple in 1997.
