## Background

- Born in Austria in 1889, Hitler had an undistinguished youth and failed as an artist in Vienna. Radicalized by WWI and the German defeat, he entered politics in Munich, building the Nazi party into a mass movement. He was appointed Chancellor in 1933 and rapidly established a dictatorship. He led Germany into World War II in 1939 and orchestrated the Holocaust. He committed suicide in Berlin in 1945 as the Third Reich collapsed.

## Motivation

- **Drives:** Driven by a social-Darwinist worldview of racial struggle, antisemitism, and a desire for German "living space" (Lebensraum) in the East.
- **Philosophy:** Believed himself to be a man of destiny, chosen by Providence to restore German greatness and destroy "Jewish Bolshevism".
- **Future Behavior Prediction:** Will continue to pursue destructive goals until stopped by force or death.

## Relationships

## Resource and Environmental Adaptation

## Long-Term Impact and Legacy Forecast

- Left a legacy of unmitigated destruction and horror. The Holocaust stands as a unique crime in history. His actions led to the division of Germany and Europe for decades. He remains the ultimate symbol of political evil.
