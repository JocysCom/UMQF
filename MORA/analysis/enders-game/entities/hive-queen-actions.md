# The Hive Queen - Actions Analysis

## Case: 1. Hive Queen - First/Second Invasions - Humanity

### Entity list

1. Humanity (Victims)
2. Bugger Species (Aggressors)

### 1 Humanity - war/death

Context: The Hive Queen attacked humanity in the First and Second Invasions, not realizing they were sentient, causing mass death.

*Note: The Buggers did not realize humans were sentient ("lonely animals who cannot dream each other's dreams"). Once they realized, they stopped.*

- ΔOS = -0.50 (Significant loss of life, existential threat, but stopped short of total destruction)
- VSA(Humanity) = 0.55
- Tc = 1.00 (Deaths are permanent)
- Vc = 1.00 (Non-consent)
- Sc = 1.00 (Warfare)

UMQ_base(a,e) = -0.50 × 0.55 × 1.00 × 2.0 × 2.0 = **-1.10**

**Responsibility & Intention:**

- PerceivedContext = Real (Misunderstanding of nature of life)
- ActualContext = Real
- At = 1.0 (Active)
- Cp = 1.0
- Ri = 1.0
- Rp = 1.0
- In = 0.1 (Unforeseeable - they did not know they were killing sentient beings; "We did not mean to murder")

UMQ_final(a,e) = -1.10 × 1.0 × 0.1 = **-0.11**
Group size: Millions (Casualties). Total = **-110,000** (Estimated moral weight of the misunderstanding).

### Aggregate results (pre-CF)

- Humanity: -110,000

### Complexity Factor adjustment

CF = 0.5 (Tragic misunderstanding between species). Adjusted Total UMQ = **-55,000**

### Actor (self) impact

- UMQ(a, Queen) ≈ -1.00 (Regret, eventual extinction).

### Summary headline

Hive Queen - First/Second Invasions - Humanity: Total UMQ = -55,000 [Highly Destructive] {Destructive}
