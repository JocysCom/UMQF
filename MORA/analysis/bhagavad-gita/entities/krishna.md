## Background

- Krishna is the Avatar of Vishnu, born to destroy evil (Kamsa, etc.) and restore Dharma. He serves as Arjuna's charioteer in the war, having vowed not to wield weapons himself.

## Motivation

- **Drives:** "For the protection of the good, for the destruction of the wicked, and for the establishment of Dharma, I come into being in every age." (Gita 4:8).
- **Philosophy:** Theism, Karma Yoga, Bhakti Yoga, Jnana Yoga. He teaches that all paths lead to him, but surrender is best.
- **Future Behavior Prediction:** Will ensure the war concludes as planned; will eventually withdraw his avatar form when his mission is complete.

## Relationships

- **Betrayal Risks:** None. He is the standard of truth.
