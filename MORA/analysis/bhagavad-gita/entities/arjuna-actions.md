# Morality Assessment: Arjuna

## Case 1. Arjuna - refusal to fight - Kauravas & Pandavas

### Entity list

1. Kaurava Army (approx. 1,000,000 warriors)
2. Pandava Army (approx. 700,000 warriors)
3. Social Order (Dharma)

### 1 Kaurava Army (1 M) - temporary survival

Context: Arjuna, overcome by pity at the sight of his kinsmen and teachers, refuses to fight, dropping his bow. This action temporarily spares the lives of the opposing army who were imminent targets of his prowess.

- ΔOS = +0.10 (temporary reprieve from death; battle is inevitable but delayed/questioned)
- VSA(Warrior)
  - Recognition 0.07 (Advanced)
  - Solving 0.09 (Advanced)
  - Simulation 0.10 (Advanced)
  - Planning 0.09 (Advanced)
  - Adaptation 0.04 (Advanced)
  - Communication 0.09 (Advanced)
  - Actions 0.10 (Complex)
  - **Total VSA = 0.58**
- Tc = 0.01 (very short duration, as battle is imminent)
- Vc = 0.00 (consent to live is assumed)
- Sc = 0.00 (no suffering caused by not fighting)

UMQ_base(per warrior) = +0.10 × 0.58 × 0.01 × 1.0 × 1.0 = **+0.00058**

**Responsibility & Intention:**

- PerceivedContext = Real
- ActualContext = Real
- At = 1.0 (Active - dropping weapons)
- Cp = 1.0 (Immediate)
- Ri = 1.0 (Unique - only he could stop his own attack)
- Rp = 1.0
- In = 1.0 (Intended to spare them)

UMQ_final(per warrior) = +0.00058 × 1.0 × 1.0 = **+0.00058**

Total for 1,000,000 warriors = +0.00058 × 1,000,000 = **+580**

### 2 Pandava Army (700,000) - risk of defeat/slaughter

Context: By refusing to fight, Arjuna risks the defeat and slaughter of his own army, as he is their key warrior.

**Responsibility & Intention:**

- PerceivedContext = Real
- ActualContext = Real

- Cp = 0.5 (Direct Consequence)
- Ri = 1.0 (Unique - his leadership is critical)
- Rp = 0.5 × 0.5 × 1.0 = **0.25**
- In = 0.5 (Foreseeable negligence)

UMQ_final(per warrior) = -0.87 × 0.25 × 0.5 = **-0.10875**

Total for 700,000 warriors = -0.10875 × 700,000 = **-76,125**

### Aggregate results (pre-CF)

- Kaurava Army: +580

### Actor (self) impact

- UMQ_expected(a, Arjuna) ≈ +0.10 (Relief from the guilt of killing kinsmen).
- UMQ_realised(a, Arjuna) ≈ -0.80 (Shame, loss of reputation, and spiritual confusion).

### Summary headline

Arjuna - refusal to fight - Kauravas & Pandavas: Total UMQ = -90,654 [Catastrophically immoral] {Destructive}
