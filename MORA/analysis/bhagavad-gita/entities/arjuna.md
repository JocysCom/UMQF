## Background

- Arjuna is the third of the Pandava brothers, son of Kunti and the god Indra. He is the greatest archer of his time, ambidextrous (Savyasacin), and the chosen friend/disciple of Krishna. He has previously spent time in exile and acquired divine weapons.

## Motivation

- **Drives:** Initially: Compassion, family loyalty, fear of sin. Later: Duty (Dharma), devotion to Krishna, desire for spiritual liberation.
- **Philosophy:** Shifts from a conventional moral view (killing kin is bad) to a Vedantic/Karmic view (action without attachment is the path to God).
- **Future Behavior Prediction:** Will execute the war with detachment; will likely seek penance or spiritual retreat after the war.

## Relationships

- **Bonds:** Krishna (Guide/Friend), Pandavas (Brothers), Draupadi (Wife), Drona/Bhisma (Teachers/Elders - conflicted).
- **Betrayal Risks:** Low. He is fiercely loyal to his brothers and Krishna.
- **Loyalty Forecast:** Absolute loyalty to Krishna and Yudhisthira.

## Resource and Environmental Adaptation

- **Survival Skills:** Elite combat skills, mastery of divine weapons, chariot warfare.
- **Tech Use:** Uses advanced weaponry (Gandiva bow, divine astras).

## Long-Term Impact and Legacy Forecast

- Arjuna's legacy is dual: the hero who restored Dharma and the instrument of a catastrophic war. His dialogue with Krishna becomes the definitive guide for Hindu ethics (The Gita), influencing billions.
