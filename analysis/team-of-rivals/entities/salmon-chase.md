## Background
- Former Governor of Ohio and US Senator. A staunch anti-slavery advocate who helped found the Republican Party. Served as Secretary of the Treasury and then Chief Justice.

## Motivation
- **Drives:** Driven by a deep religious conviction against slavery and an equally deep conviction that he was destined to be President.
- **Philosophy:** Believes in "natural right" and the moral obligation of the government to ensure freedom.
- **Future Behavior Prediction:** Will continue to seek the presidency until his death; will serve as a principled Chief Justice.

## Relationships
- **Abraham Lincoln:** Strained. Chase respected Lincoln's goodness but doubted his ability; Lincoln respected Chase's ability but disliked his maneuvering.
- **Kate Chase:** Intense, almost co-dependent relationship with his ambitious daughter, who managed his social life and political aspirations.
- **Edwin Stanton:** Close friend and ally, though the relationship shifted as Stanton grew closer to Lincoln.

## Resource and Environmental Adaptation
- **Survival Skills:** Intellectual resilience; ability to work long hours.
- **Tech Use:** Modernized the financial system (greenbacks, national banks).

## Long-Term Impact and Legacy Forecast
- Remembered as a great Treasury Secretary and Chief Justice. His face on the $10,000 bill (and $1 bill) cements his financial legacy.
